About me


I've been born in 1999, in a small city in Romania called Piatra Neam»õ. Even though I've been drawing my whole life, 2018 was the year I started professionally creating artworks.

During this endeavour, I quickly became fascinated about how the human imagination works and how it offers you endless possibilities of expression; I started painting canvases, continuously developing my general visual style as well as various forms of applying it. (Canvas, Paper, Murals, etc.)

In 2021, I began learning about Web3 and started shaping The Circle as a community of artists & art collectors, as well as other types of designers (3D, Sound, Apparel, etc.) which are interested in the revolution of Blockchain technology.

Artwork Features

Physical Art


Each artwork of mine is a Physical Hand-Painted Canvas and has a layer of protection called Varnish which increases it's overall durability.

NFT Ownership


Every single canvas signed by StefanB has it's Certificate of Authenticity stored on Ethereum Blockchain, a highly secured Web3 technology.

HQ Digitization


Also, each such NFT is created by using a High-Quality Digitization of the physical canvas, which offer the owner various possibilities of reproducing & digitally exhibiting his artwork.
